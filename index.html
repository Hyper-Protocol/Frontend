Token Burn


Every 24 hours, 0.35% of the HYPER in the liqudity pool is burned, directly increasing the price on Pancakeswap.

Transaction Tax


On every transaction, a 2.5% tax is sent to an staking wallet, providing rewards for liqudity pool stakers.

Staking dApp


Aside from providing rewards for liqudity pool stakers, it also sends BNB directly to the liquidity pool to increase the price.
